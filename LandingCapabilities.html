Core modules that power your operations

Each module is purpose-built for high-volume contact centers. They connect seamlessly but can roll out independently to match the maturity of each campaign.

Scheduling

Shift orchestration

Balance coverage with predictive forecasts, automate shift swaps, and trigger exception workflows directly through Google Workspace.

  • Scenario planning with interval forecasts and adherence overlays.
  • Automated swap approvals, overtime guardrails, and manager alerts.
  • Work-from-home readiness checks tied to equipment and network audits.
Coaching

Enablement playbooks

Give supervisors guided workflows to assign action plans, capture feedback, and celebrate wins in one place.

  • Custom coaching templates with digital sign-offs and attachments.
  • Performance heatmaps that highlight the biggest coaching opportunities.
  • Automated nudges that keep follow-ups and reinforcements on schedule.
Quality

QA intelligence hub

Aggregate evaluator feedback, calibrate teams, and surface QA performance trends with a modern reporting layer.

  • Weighted scoring with variance alerts across lines of business.
  • Calibration dashboards that preserve historical context and disputes.
  • AI-ready exports that feed deeper analytics or generative summaries.

Coordinate cross-functional projects, document requirements, and drive accountability across partner teams.
