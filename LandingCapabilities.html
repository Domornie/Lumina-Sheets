Capability pillars

Start with the core pillars that frame LuminaHQ implementations.

Quality excellence

Measure what matters with calibrations, collaborative reviews, and fast publishing.

  • Quality dashboards with drillable filters and campaign rollups.
  • Comment threading and coaching triggers within QA evaluations.
  • Export templates ready for executive briefings.

Coaching lifecycle

Plan, deliver, and confirm coaching conversations end-to-end.

  • Guided coaching forms tied to agent scorecards.
  • Notifications and check-ins keep action items on track.
  • Digital acknowledgments secure agent sign-off.

Workforce orchestration

Give workforce teams the visibility to balance coverage and compliance.

  • Schedule management with proactive conflict resolution.
  • Attendance trackers with import utilities and alerts.
  • Forecast inputs align to QA and coaching capacity.

Enablement accelerators

Capabilities that keep your teams informed, compliant, and empowered.

Knowledge and policy

Centralized knowledge hubs hold SOPs, change logs, and policy alerts.

  • In-app documentation tied directly to operational modules.
  • Announcement banners and notifications keep teams current.

Collaboration fabric

Threads, mentions, and task integrations deliver shared accountability.

  • Deep links from QA records to escalations and chats.
  • Cross-functional dashboards show handoffs across teams.

Analytics readiness

Data pipelines and exports keep leadership and AI co-pilots informed.

  • Structured exports for BI tools and GPT-style assistants.
  • Snapshot metrics highlight performance at-a-glance.

Security & governance

Guardrails support compliance for regulated industries and enterprise clients.

  • Role-based navigation and safe defaults for every module.
  • Audit logs for evaluations, acknowledgments, and exports.
