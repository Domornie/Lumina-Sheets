Why we exist

Call center leaders are asked to coach, staff, report, and innovate without friction. LuminaHQ removes the swivel-chair work by giving teams an integrated workspace that adapts to each campaign, geography, and client requirement.

Customer-first design

Every workflow is tested with active operations teams to ensure the UI stays intuitive even for fast-scaling programs.

Human-centered automation

Automation is only useful when it empowers analysts and supervisors. Our scripts reduce manual steps while keeping people in control.

Secure collaboration

Multi-tenant controls, audit trails, and granular permissions protect customer data while keeping teams aligned.

Global reach, local roots

We support distributed teams across the Americas, yet maintain our product craft and culture in the Caribbean.

How LuminaHQ evolved

A cross-functional team of engineers, data analysts, and operations specialists continues to expand LuminaHQ based on real-world call center use cases.

2019

Scheduling foundations

Introduced the first Google Workspace scripts to automate agent shift updates and broadcast daily coaching plans.

2021

Unified coaching

Expanded into coaching dashboards, QA data pipelines, and collaboration workflows for hybrid teams.

2023

Enterprise-ready security

Shipped tenant-aware access controls, SSO alignment, and audit logging for compliance-driven partners.

Today

Predictive intelligence

Integrating forecasting, AI summarization, and proactive alerts so teams anticipate changes instead of reacting to them.

Inside the LuminaHQ culture

We operate with curiosity, empathy, and a bias toward shipping. Our product rituals keep customer teams connected to the builders shaping their tools.

  • Weekly design reviews align engineering, QA, and enablement on new feature experiments.
  • Immersive onboarding embeds our product team directly inside partner operations for live feedback.
  • Community initiatives reinvest in Jamaican tech talent through mentorship, internships, and open-source learning.
  • Dedicated customer pods pair product managers with operations leads for faster roadmap alignment.
  • We balance rapid delivery with sustainable workloads to maintain a resilient team.
