LuminaHQ incorporates multiple layers of security to protect you and your organization while browsing the web.

Every website you visit goes through our security filtering system:

Each website is checked against our database of known malicious and blocked domains before allowing access.

Content Filtering

Website content is scanned for malicious code, inappropriate material, and security threats.

Safe Delivery

Clean, filtered content is delivered to your browser through our secure proxy system.

Blocked Content

Certain types of content are automatically blocked for security:

Frequently Asked Questions

Why can't I access certain websites? +

Websites may be blocked for several reasons:

  • Security Risk: The site contains malware or phishing content
  • Policy Violation: The site violates your organization's acceptable use policy
  • Content Category: The site falls into a blocked category (e.g., social media, gaming)
  • Geographic Restrictions: The site blocks access from your region

If you need access to a blocked site for legitimate business purposes, contact your IT administrator with a business justification.

Can I download files through LuminaHQ? +

File downloads are restricted for security purposes. Most executable files (.exe, .bat, .cmd) are automatically blocked. Document downloads (PDF, DOCX, XLSX) may be allowed depending on your organization's policy.

If you need to download a specific file, contact your IT administrator who can review the request and provide the file through secure channels if appropriate.

How do I report a security concern? +

If you encounter suspicious content or potential security threats:

  1. Immediately close the website tab
  2. Note the website URL and describe what you observed
  3. Report the incident to your IT security team via your organization's standard reporting process
  4. Do not attempt to investigate further or share the suspicious link with colleagues

Your organization's security team will investigate and take appropriate action.

Why are some pages loading slowly? +

Page loading speed can be affected by several factors:

  • Security Scanning: All content is scanned for threats, which adds processing time
  • Content Filtering: Large amounts of content may require additional processing
  • Network Conditions: Your internet connection speed affects loading times
  • Website Complexity: Sites with many resources take longer to process

The system uses caching to improve performance for frequently visited sites. If a specific site consistently loads slowly, report it to IT for investigation.

Can I use LuminaHQ on my personal device? +

LuminaHQ access depends on your organization's security policy. Generally:

  • Company Devices: Full access is typically provided
  • Personal Devices: May require additional security software or VPN connection
  • Mobile Devices: Often supported with appropriate mobile device management

Check with your IT department about personal device access policies and requirements.

How long are my bookmarks and browsing history kept? +

Data retention policies vary by organization:

  • Bookmarks: Typically retained as long as your account is active
  • Browsing History: Usually kept for compliance purposes (often 7 years)
  • Search Queries: Logged for security analysis and policy compliance

Your organization's privacy policy will outline specific retention periods. Contact your privacy officer for detailed information.

What should I do if LuminaHQ is not working? +

Follow these troubleshooting steps:

  1. Refresh the page: Use Ctrl+F5 (Cmd+Shift+R on Mac) to force refresh
  2. Clear browser cache: Clear your browser's cache and cookies
  3. Try a different browser: Test with Chrome, Firefox, or Edge
  4. Check network connection: Ensure you have internet connectivity
  5. Restart browser: Close and reopen your web browser

If issues persist, contact your IT help desk with details about the problem, including any error messages you're seeing.
