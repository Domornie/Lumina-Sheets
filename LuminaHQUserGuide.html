First Time Access

Access LuminaHQ

Open your organization's LuminaHQ URL. Most teams access the workspace through a Google Workspace link such as https://scripts.luminahq.com/?campaign=XYZ.

Authenticate with SSO

Sign in using your Google Workspace or federated credentials. Multi-factor prompts mirror your organization's default policies.

Select your workspace

Choose the campaign, site, or client workspace you manage. Your role determines which modules appear by default.

Confirm preferences

Set notification preferences, connect any authorized data sources, and verify your default dashboards.

Pin the LuminaHQ app in Chrome (right-click the tab → Pin) and add it to your Google Workspace app launcher for one-click access during every shift.

Frequently Asked Questions

How is the command center different from the legacy browser view? +

The legacy experience focused on secure web browsing. The command center unifies workforce management, quality, performance, and automation in a single workspace. You still benefit from enterprise security, but now the emphasis is on orchestrating operations end-to-end.

Where do I find automation logs and approvals? +

Open the Automation Studio module and check the Run History tab. Each entry lists the initiator, impacted records, and approval path. Filter by campaign or date to review specific runs.

What should I do if metrics look incorrect? +

Confirm you are viewing the correct campaign context. Then compare the dashboard snapshot to raw data exports or source systems. If the discrepancy persists, open a data quality ticket via the support workflow with screenshots and time ranges.

How can I request a new module or dashboard? +

Use the Request Enhancement option in the action drawer. Provide the business objective, required data sources, and desired audience. Product operations reviews submissions weekly.

Can frontline agents access the same command center? +

Frontline views are streamlined dashboards or task lists tailored to their role. Agents do not see leadership-only modules unless granted temporary access through governance workflows.

How long are session recaps retained? +

Session recaps follow your compliance retention schedule‚Äîtypically 14 days for layouts and 7 years for decision logs and automation history. Check the Governance dashboard for campaign-specific policies.

What happens if a scheduled export fails? +

You will see an alert in the notification center and the export status will change to Blocked. Review the failure details, adjust the request, or escalate to the governance team for assistance.
