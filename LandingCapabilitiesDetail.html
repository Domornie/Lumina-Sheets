How the modules connect

Follow the orchestration path from insight capture to agent enablement.

1. Observe & evaluate

Quality analysts capture evaluations with rich context and standardized scoring.

  • Guided forms collect customer impact, root cause, and policy notes.
  • Collaboration widgets invite calibrations before publishing.
  • Evaluations flow to dashboards that highlight trend outliers.

2. Coach & acknowledge

Supervisors turn QA insights into action while agents stay looped in at every step.

  • Coaching forms map improvement plans and celebrate wins.
  • Agents sign digital acknowledgments that confirm understanding.
  • Automated reminders surface pending follow-ups before deadlines.

3. Optimize & forecast

Workforce leaders blend attendance, scheduling, and coaching commitments to stay ahead of demand.

  • Shared calendars display staffing coverage alongside coaching events.
  • Data exports feed BI layers or AI copilots for advanced modeling.
  • Governance controls ensure only authorized leaders can trigger changes.

Workflow deep dives

Key processes showing how teams collaborate inside LuminaHQ.

Quality calibration loop

Align criteria before evaluations impact scoring.

  • Analysts submit sample evaluations for leadership review.
  • Calibration notes sync to scoring rubrics automatically.
  • Finalized standards publish directly to QA forms.

Coaching follow-through

Ensure accountability after every coaching conversation.

  • Action items assigned to agents with due dates and owners.
  • Links to knowledge resources and policy updates embedded in context.
  • Progress tracked across dashboards for leaders and QA partners.

Attendance harmonization

Keep workforce, QA, and coaching schedules synchronized.

  • Imports normalize data from biometric or WFM systems.
  • Alerts notify leaders when adherence or compliance thresholds slip.
  • Joint reviews prioritize coverage for coaching-critical agents.

Executive insights

Provide leadership with a cohesive view of operations health.

  • Dashboards blend QA, CSAT, staffing, and escalation data.
  • AI-ready summaries convert metrics into key talking points.
  • Shareable packets export for governance and partner reviews.

Implementation blueprint

Use these steps to align stakeholders and launch successfully.

Kickoff & discovery

  • Workshops gather current QA, coaching, and WFM processes.
  • Define campaign groupings, roles, and navigation access.
  • Consolidate templates, scripts, and policies for import.

Configuration sprint

  • Tailor evaluation rubrics, coaching forms, and notification cadences.
  • Map integrations and schedule data refresh windows.
  • Build enablement assets for analysts and supervisors.

Pilot & iterate

  • Select pilot teams across QA, coaching, and workforce functions.
  • Collect baseline metrics to measure improvement.
  • Incorporate feedback and finalize go-live runbooks.

Launch & expand

  • Deploy modules with coordinated communications.
  • Monitor adoption dashboards and respond to support signals.
  • Extend automations and analytics layers as teams mature.
