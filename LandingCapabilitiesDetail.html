Detailed module playbooks

These summaries expand on the modules you explored previously. They draw from the same scheduling, QA, coaching, and automation capabilities already described across the LuminaHQ landing pages—now packaged with configuration guidance.

Adaptive scheduling

Balance staffing in real time with forecasting overlays, automated shift swaps, and instant communications to every affected agent.

  • Rolling interval forecasts fused with attendance trends for proactive staffing.
  • Exception workflows that loop in workforce, HR, and operations leads.
  • Native notifications and approvals aligned to Google Chat and Gmail.

QA intelligence

Aggregate evaluator feedback, calibrate teams, and surface QA performance trends with a modern reporting layer.

  • Weighted scorecards with variance alerts across lines of business.
  • Calibration dashboards that preserve historical context and disputes.
  • AI-ready exports for deeper analytics or generative summaries.

Coaching orchestration

Give supervisors guided workflows to assign action plans, capture feedback, and celebrate wins in one place.

  • Performance heatmaps highlight the biggest coaching opportunities.
  • Templates with digital sign-offs and attachments to keep everyone aligned.
  • Automated nudges ensure follow-ups and reinforcements stay on schedule.

Automation studio

Trigger workflows across Sheets, Gmail, and external APIs with reusable recipes that shrink manual effort and accelerate resolution.

  • Drag-and-drop flow builder with guardrails and approvals.
  • Secure integrations and webhook triggers to keep systems synced.
  • SLA-aware automations that escalate before targets are missed.
